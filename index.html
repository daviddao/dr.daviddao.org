Dr. David Dao
david@gainforest.net
Co-Founder & Chief Scientist, GainForest
I'm the co-founder and chief scientist of GainForest, a decentralized science non-profit working on emerging technologies for Nature & Climate.

GainForest is a non-profit grantee of Microsoft's AI for Earth program and a winner of the $10M XPRIZE Rainforest Competition. We develop technology to tackle the climate and biodiversity crisis. My doctoral dissertation at ETH Zurich advanced algorithms and applications for machine learning-based data valuation, and I completed postdoctoral research in environmental and computer science there. I also maintain Github's most starred collection on ethical use of AI and I'm one of the co-authors of Tensorflow Models. Previously, I conducted research at Berkeley AI Research (BAIR), Stanford University, and the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard, and worked as an engineer in Silicon Valley. I'm a Global Shaper (Davos 50) at World Economic Forum, a Core Member of Climate Change AI, a Climate Leader at Climate Reality Project, a Mentor at Creative Destruction Lab, a United Nations delegate at COP (since COP23 Bonn), and have organized conferences with thousands of attendees in Germany, Silicon Valley, and at Harvard.
